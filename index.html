About

Xinlei Chen is an Associate Professor with Tsinghua University SIGS. He received his Ph.D. degree from Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Prof. Pei Zhang.

His work uses multi-agent collaborative strategies to solve real problems in smart cities and industries on city-level large-scale AIoT platforms. His research spans cyber physical systems, Artificial Internet of Things, ubiquitous and mobile computing, smart sensing, wireless communication and networks, and embodied intelligent systems.
